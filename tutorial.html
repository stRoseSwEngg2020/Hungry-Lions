~Tutorial & Rules~

-You are an explorer trying to find long lost treasure underground.

-You must traverse a 2-D grid until you find the treasure or run out of health points.

-":)" marks where you are currently standing on the grid.

-"?" represents unexplored territory. You might find the treasure...or trigger a mine instead! You will lose HP if you land on a mine.

-"X" spaces mark the spaces you already checked.

-"[ ]" represents an empty space. These spaces hold neither treasure nor trap, but they lower the number of spaces you must check in each level.

-[$] the treasure is hidden in a random "?" space. Find it in three levels in a row to win!

-Click a box on the grid to guess where the treasure is hidden.

-Click 'Quit Game' to stop playing and return to the menu.

-Scoring-

EASY Mode: 500 + (Health x2) per level

MEDIUM Mode: 600 + (Health x2.5) per level

HARD Mode: 800 + (Health x2) per level
